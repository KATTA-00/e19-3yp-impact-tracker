Introduction


Our project tackles the critical issue of head injuries in sports by developing a groundbreaking impact tracking system.
Our soultion:

  • Real-time impact tracking via a wearable device attached to the head guard.
  • User-friendly desktop dashboard for data visualization
  • Cumulative Analysis of Head Impacts
  • Other Performance Metrics tracking

Impax represents a significant step forward in protecting the health and well-being of athletes across contact sports. By providing accurate and accessible information, we strive to empower a new era of safer sports environments where athletes can perform at their best without jeopardizing their long-term health.

Problem

Problem

"The study examined 694 brains, and found that 20.2% of former athletes had Lewy Body Disease." - The Tennessean
“An extra year of rugby increases the risk of CTE by 14%”- BBC

Growing awareness of the long-term consequences of repeated head impacts in sports has revealed a high prevalence of brain disorders among former athletes, emphasizing the need for proactive monitoring. Subconcussive impacts, even without immediate symptoms, contribute to long-term brain damage. The absence of a reliable and affordable impact-monitoring system poses challenges in identifying at-risk athletes and developing effective preventative measures.

  • Prevalence of Brain Disorders:
        Former athletes show a concerning prevalence of brain disorders like LBD and     CTE due to repeated head impacts
  • Subconcussive Impacts and Long-Term Damage:
        Regular subconcussive impacts, especially in contact sports, contribute to     long-term brain damage.
  • Challenges in Monitoring:
        Lack of a reliable method makes it difficult to identify at-risk athletes and     implement preventive measures.
  • Limitations of Current Systems:
        Current impact-monitoring systems are either expensive or lack the needed     depth for comprehensive analysis.
  • Call for a Reliable and Affordable Solution:
        There is a critical need for an affordable and reliable impact-monitoring     system applicable to both contact and non-contact sports.
Our Solution

Our Solution

In response to the escalating concern surrounding long-term brain disorders resulting from repeated head impacts in sports, ImpaX emerges as a groundbreaking impact tracking system. This innovative solution addresses the challenges posed by subconcussive impacts, providing reliable monitoring and preventive measures.

  • Real-time Impact Tracking:
    Wearable device for continuous, real-time monitoring during athletic activities.
  • User-Friendly Dashboard:
    Intuitive desktop dashboard offering quick insights into athletes' performance metrics.
  • Cumulative Analysis:
    Conducts cumulative analysis of head impacts, providing a historical perspective for tailored safety strategies.
  • Cost-Effective Solution:
    Affordable impact-monitoring system applicable to both contact and non-contact sports.
  • Ongoing Improvement:
    Continuously refines data communication and security measures to meet the highest standards.
